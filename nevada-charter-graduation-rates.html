Public Working Artifact

Nevada Charter Graduation Rates

This page summarizes a public, reproducible estimate of Nevada charter-sector 4-year graduation rates, paired with official statewide and district comparison rates from Nevada's annual ACGR releases.

For a shorter narrative version, start with the overview page.

What This Is

The official public rows come directly from Nevada Department of Education graduation-rate releases. The charter slices are estimated from school-level public records, cleaned through a school-year decision ledger and grade-12 enrollment cross-checks.

Historical authorizer is tied to the reporting year. Later sponsor transfers do not rewrite earlier data.

Key Rules

  • Some historical NDE sponsor reporting appears to have lumped non-charter entities such as `Davidson` / `University Schools` and `Independence` / the correctional LEA into SPCSA public totals. This project therefore keeps the official SPCSA line as published but also shows an adjusted charter-only SPCSA line that excludes those entities.
  • `SPCSA Alternative Schools` exists to separate schools operating in a different accountability, mission, and political environment from traditional brick-and-mortar charters. `Beacon` is still highlighted within that slice because its APF treatment makes it an especially important school to watch.
  • `SPCSA Virtual` exists for the same reason: statewide online schools and online-adjacent operators face a different policy and operating environment than traditional campuses. Breaking them out makes structural differences visible instead of hiding them inside one blended SPCSA average.
  • The school-by-school drilldowns are there both to let readers check the math and to show that operator behavior changed over time inside those structural categories.
  • District-operated option schools such as NV Learning Academy and North Star Online are excluded because they were not charter schools under Nevada charter law.
  • Suppressed top-coded rows use interval treatment: + becomes 95.0 to 100.0 with midpoint 97.5.
  • Suppressed bottom-coded rows use interval treatment: * becomes 0.0 to 5.0 with midpoint 2.5.
  • If enrollment files show no 12th grade and there is no contrary published ACGR row, the school-year is excluded from the 4-year graduation universe.
  • Democracy Prep is treated as an Achievement School District school for 2017-18 and 2018-19, then as an SPCSA school afterward.

Coverage Status

The modern years are now mostly resolved. The main remaining modern hold is a tiny suppressed 2018-19 Nevada State High School Meadowwood cohort.
